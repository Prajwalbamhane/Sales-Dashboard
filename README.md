Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and visualize business sales performance, profitability trends, customer behavior, and product insights. The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making through dynamic KPIs, trend analysis, and interactive filtering capabilities.

Short Description / Purpose

The Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard is a business intelligence solution developed using Power BI to help organizations monitor sales operations, track profit trends, and evaluate overall business performance. The dashboard provides interactive visualizations that support strategic decision-making for sales teams, managers, and business analysts.

⚙️ Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 Power BI Desktop – Main data visualization platform used to build interactive business reports and dashboards.
• 📂 Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing workflows.
• 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for calculated measures, KPIs, aggregations, and business logic.
• 📈 Data Modeling – Relationships established between sales-related tables to enable filtering, drilldowns, and dynamic reporting.
• 📉 Interactive Visualizations – Includes bar charts, donut charts, KPI cards, slicers, and trend analysis visuals.
• 📁 PBIX File Format – Used for Power BI dashboard development and report sharing.

Features / Highlights
• Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to monitor sales performance efficiently due to scattered business data and lack of centralized reporting systems. Without proper analytics dashboards, identifying profit trends, underperforming products, or seasonal sales patterns becomes time-consuming and inefficient.

Key business challenges include:

Difficulty tracking monthly sales performance
Limited visibility into profit trends
Inefficient reporting processes
Lack of centralized KPI monitoring
Slow business decision-making due to fragmented data
• Goal of the Dashboard

The primary objective of this dashboard is to provide a centralized and interactive sales analytics solution that:

Tracks business sales performance in real time
Monitors profitability trends across time periods
Identifies top-performing products or categories
Supports management reporting and strategic planning
Enables faster and more informed business decisions
